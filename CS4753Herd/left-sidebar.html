About Us

Welcome to Herd!

Our founders are a group of intrepid students from UVa who dreamed of an easy way to organize get-togethers with friends and fellow students alike. We looked through the available apps and decided we wanted to bring students together in a more personalized way, with increased accessibility and functionality.

Herd is designed to make spontaneous and short-warning plans painless and possible. Never again be stuck without a frisbee buddy for that study break you manage to squeeze in, a tennis partner on a Saturday afternoon, a new Super Smash competitor, or a jam session crew when you’re jonesing to play your cajon.

Getting Started

Start building a profile by linking your Facebook or .edu email address and go from there! Add a picture, what you’re interested in, and any info about you, boo. You can select friends who have the app from your phone and Facebook, see their updates, and choose whether you see other nearby students’ event suggestions. Then it’s up to you! Select activities to create or join based on category, number of people needed, time, and location- perfect for making plans a week in advance, a day, or even half an hour. You can even set recurring events if you’ve got that special perfect-scheduling spunk.

Sign up today to receive something other apps don’t give you: crowdsourced community that’s convenient, organized, and optimized. Whether you fly by the seat of your pants or already know what you’re doing this weekend, we’ve got you covered.
